About Us

Welcome to the official Room Booking System of the Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP) — a modern, digital solution developed to simplify and streamline the process of reserving university spaces.

Our Story

At RUPP, we understand the importance of accessible, well-managed spaces for learning, collaboration, and academic events. This platform was created to help students, faculty, and staff easily book rooms for group study, meetings, workshops, and university events. Our mission is to make room booking simple, transparent, and fair for everyone at RUPP.

Why Use Our System?

Fast & Easy

Book a room in just a few clicks. Our user-friendly interface makes the process quick and hassle-free for everyone.

Fair & Transparent

See real-time room availability and prevent double-bookings. Everyone gets equal access to university spaces.

Secure & Reliable

Your bookings and data are safe. We use secure technology to protect your information and ensure reliability.

This room booking system is part of our ongoing effort to improve digital access and resource management at RUPP.

Thank you for using our system responsibly!
